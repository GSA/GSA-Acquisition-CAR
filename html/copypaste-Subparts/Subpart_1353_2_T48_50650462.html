Subpart 1353.2 - Prescription of Forms

Parent topic: PART 1353 - FORMS

1353.200 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes or references DOC forms for use in acquisitions. Consistent with FAR 53.200, this subpart is arranged by subject matter, in the same order as and keyed to the parts of the CAR in which the form usage requirements are addressed.

1353.206 Competition requirements.

As prescribed in 48 CFR 1306.303-70, use Form CD-492, Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition, to support the requirements under FAR Subpart 6.3 (see Appendix A: Forms).

1353.219 Small business programs.

Use Form CD-570, Small Business Set-Aside Review, to fulfill and document the requirements under FAR 19.5 (see Appendix A: Forms).
