1352.242-70 Postaward conference.

As prescribed in 48 CFR 1342.503-70, insert the following provision:

Postaward Conference (APR 2010)

A postaward conference with the successful offeror may be required. If required, the Contracting Officer will contact the contractor within 10 days of contract award to arrange the conference.

(End of clause)
