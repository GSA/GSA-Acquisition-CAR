1370.102 Pre-bid/pre-proposal conference and site visit.

Insert provision 1352.270-71, Pre-Bid/Pre-Proposal Conference and Site Visit, in solicitations where a pre-proposal conference will be held. The provision is optional for construction and may be modified as necessary. The contracting officer shall include or delete the paragraph regarding site visits.
