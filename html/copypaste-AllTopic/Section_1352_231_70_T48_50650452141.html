1352.231-70 Precontract costs.

As prescribed in 48 CFR 1331.205-32, insert the following clause:

Precontract Costs (APR 2010)

The contractor is entitled to reimbursement for allowable, allocable, and reasonable costs incurred during the period of ________ to the award date of this contract in an amount not to exceed $________.

(End of clause)
